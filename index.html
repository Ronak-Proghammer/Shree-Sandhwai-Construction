About US

Shree Sindhwai Construction group was founded in 2015 by Mr. Uttam Kanaiyalal Patel. Utilizing the incredibly broad and diverse experience of our executive partners, the major goal of the Shree Sindhwai Construction is to take on and carry out civil engineering projects of any scale and character while upholding the highest standard of quality. We are a group of Engineers and Contractors. Hitesh Engineers and Anant Associates were the two partnership businesses that existed historically. The first business's foundation was laid in 2003, and more recently, the latter was founded in 2009. Shree Sindhwai Construction was established as a sole proprietor firm in 2015.

Services

A multifunctional construction firm in India, Shree Sindhwai Construction provides a wide variety of construction and its related services for industrial projects. Alongside, the company is also involved in various site development projects. It offers its services to non-governmental sector across the whole construction value chain, from planning and design to building and post-construction operations, including MEP work and other interior fit outs.

Industrial Construction

Building Industries and dealing with manufacturing, power generation, distribution, and other highly specialised enterprises. Working on the mechanical and structural parts of these facilities, as well as their design, installation, and maintenance.

Drainage Systems Construction

In civil engineering and construction projects, drainage is frequently a crucial component that must be included if flooding and other damages are to be prevented. And this includes sewers, fitting slit pipes and treatment facilities.

Institutional Construction

Any structure that performs a function in the fields of healthcare, education, recreation, or public works is referred to as an institutional building. We specialise in building universities, hospitals, and elementary schools.

Site Development

Site development entails planning and obtaining permits for all exterior improvements. Removals, site grading, sanitary and storm sewers, access and asphalt, parking, and utility connections for gas, electric, and water are all included in this.
